ABOUT US

The team at Onnes comprises of the best talented Scientists and Engineers who are motivated and committed to build world-class cryogenic products. Onnes is founded with the core principle to become a world leader in cryocooler technology. The goal of Onnes is to design and manufacture a wide range of cryocoolers for space, military, defense, and commercial industries. Onnes Cryogenics conducts cutting-edge research to advance cryocooler technology adaptable to the harshest and rugged environmental conditions addressing the strategic needs of our customers.
